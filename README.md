## Project_2 My Grades
LLM used for this project Claude AI. A sheet of 20 students with details of all exams in Statistics was created. The grades and percentage achieved in each subject were calculated through AI after giving appropriate weightage to all elements of the subject and defining the grading criterion. The result was presented in the form of a report attached to the project. 
## Prompt
The attached reports contain the marks of the Statistics subject of 20 students. Calculate the % age and grades of each student; ignore the outliers, give 7% to assignments, 8 % to the project, 15 % to quizzes, 25 & to the midterm, and 45 % to the final exam. Also give grades 50-55 % = C- , 55-60 = C+ and so on. Create a beautiful/downable final report
